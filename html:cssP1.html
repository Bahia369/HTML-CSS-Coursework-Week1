Summary

Amandip is the founder of Dhol Frequency, currently aged at 35 years old Amandip also known as Aman Dholi as his nickname is Aman started off his passion for music at the age of 10 when he was taught how to play the tabla, dholki and used to to attend the local gudwara every morning with his grand mother and Amandip would do the seva of the table in the kirtan this was a daily routine for years for Aman. At the age of 13 Aman had also been taught how to play the dhol and done his first show at the age of 13 with Desi-rythems Dj Bally k.
